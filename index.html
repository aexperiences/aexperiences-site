Questions, answered straight

What you're probably wondering.

Do I own the hub?
You own your content, your data, and your brand outright, and it runs on your own domain. We build, host, and maintain the hub for a flat monthly — no lock-in games, and your data is yours to export.
Are there per-seat or per-transaction fees?
No per-transaction fee — you connect your own payment processor and keep 100% of your sales. Extra seats are a flat $18 per user per month, only if you need them.
What if my industry isn't listed?
The seven shown are just where we've already done the build. The hub adapts to most operations — tell us what you run and we'll scope a custom one.
How long does it take to launch?
Most hubs go live within a few weeks. We start with the smallest version that works on your real data, prove it, then expand — so you see something real early.
Can I move off my current software?
Yes. Data migration starts at $1,500 and we bring your existing records over, so you're not starting from a blank slate.
Do you offer non-profit pricing?
Yes — 30% off monthly for mission-driven organizations, plus founder/early-adopter (20% off for life + half off the build) and annual-prepay (two months free) programs.
