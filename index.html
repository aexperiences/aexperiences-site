Who we are

A father, a nurse, and four kids who keep us honest.

Accelerated Experiences is a real family business, not a brand exercise — here's who's actually behind it.

Anthony Esposito spent two decades in data-driven marketing before he built any software — starting as a marketing analyst at a New York Times Media Group newspaper, then founding an analytics department from scratch across three papers, then leading marketing and operations for over a decade in senior living and hospice care, including running a full retirement community as General Manager: P&L, census, referral networks, real operational command. That instinct — read the audience, target the message, own the numbers — is what now drives every platform, brand, and campaign this studio builds. Today he designs and builds complete digital businesses end to end: software platforms, branded websites and apps, marketing systems, and the pricing and sales infrastructure behind them.

Jessica Esposito, RN, is Accelerated Experiences' co-founder. She's a full-time registered nurse with over a decade of experience spanning Labor & Delivery to geriatric home health and hospice care, and has served as a Director of Nursing. The same instinct for care, calm judgment, and getting the details right under pressure runs through how this studio treats every client.

Anthony and Jessica live in Post Falls, Idaho, with their four children — ranging from eight months to twelve years old — who keep both of them honest about building things worth doing well.
