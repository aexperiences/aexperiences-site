Questions, answered straight

What you’re probably wondering.

Do I own the hub?
You own your content, your data, and your brand outright, and it runs on your own domain. We build, host, and maintain the Cartwheel Hub for a flat monthly — no lock-in, and your data is yours to export.
Are there per-seat or per-transaction fees?
No per-transaction fee — you connect your own payment processor and keep 100% of your revenue. Extra seats are a flat $18 per user per month, only if you need them.
Is it really built for kids activity gym?
Yes. The Cartwheel Hub is configured to how kids activity gym actually works — not a generic template. We tailor it to your business before it goes live.
How long does it take to launch?
Most hubs go live within a few weeks. We start with the smallest version that works on your real data, prove it, then expand — so you see something real early.
Can I move off my current software?
Yes. Data migration starts at $1,500 and we bring your existing records over, so you are not starting from a blank slate.
Do you offer non-profit pricing?
Yes — 30% off monthly for mission-driven organizations, plus founder (20% off for life + 50% off the build) and annual-prepay (2 months free) programs.
