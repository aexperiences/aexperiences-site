Privacy Policy

The only personal information we receive is what you choose to send when you email us (your email address and whatever you write). We use it to reply to you and for nothing else. This website is intended for adults — parents, learners, and business owners. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has sent us personal information, email us and we'll delete it.
